ABOUT A.Y.I SHARED BOOKMARK

A.Y.I Bookmark is a shared bookmarking platform created by Angela, Yan, and Ifeoma as part of our midterm project.

As developers in training, we're constantly discovering useful articles and resources online. We wanted a simple way to save those links, organize them, and share them with others.That became A.Y.I Bookmark, which is a straightforward and collaborative space where users can create, view, and interact with bookmarked links.

Working together, we combined our ideas and strengths to build a platform that allows users to save bookmarks with titles and descriptions, like and copy links, and browse content in reverse chronological order.

It's simple, practical, and built with teamwork.
